About us

The concept of Madabit is to allow people from all around the world to be able to socialize while gaming without placing too much focus on the game. We emphasize on socializing, interaction, and gaining profits in the process. The intention of the game is to create teamwork, as they team up to beat the game, gain profits, and trade their cryptocurrencies among each other. Each game is unique and complex because the algorithm behind this game is based on blockchain technology which makes it fair for players and the Company alike. The outcome of each match is random, and neither the player or Company is able to manipulate it. Players feel confident, knowing that the game cannot be rigged, and the Company is secure, knowing the platform is not vulnerable and safe from outside influence. Every result is a backed up with hash, proving that each result is randomly generated, and not predetermined. Not only are the results random & fair, we have a dedicated team monitoring the system 24 hours for errors or glitches which may cause the game to be unbalanced. We also use state-of-the-art servers to ensure the least latency and smoothness in the game. Our well trained VIP customer service support teams are there to assist in your enquiries. Join us today!

How to play?

In order to play, you need to have Bits in your account.
Obtain Bits by purchasing through us or exchanging your Bitcoin/Ethereum. Once Bits are obtained, you are ready to play the game. Place your preferred amount of Bits before the end of the 5 second countdown. As the multiplier increases, you can choose at any time to exit, and turn a profit. You lose your placed Bits if multiplier "Busts" before you exit.
Note: Multiplier begins increasing from 1x value.

 What Is Extra Bet?

Extra Bet is to hedge against the multiplier result being 0. Due to the randomness of the result, it is unknown when and what the multiplier result is, when 0 result will appear and how often it appears. Extra Bet hedges against this as you as a player are allowed to place a small amount to recover your loses in the event multiplier result is 0.

What are bits?

A "Bit" is equivalent to a 100 for each US dollar. The amount of “Bits” you receive when exchanging Bitcoin/Ethereum depends on the set exchange rates. Exchange rates maybe different everyday according to market supply and demand and is subject to market fluctuations.
At the current time of writing, one Bitcoin is worth USD9,330 and Etherum is worth USD727. If you exchange your Bitcoin/Ethereum you will obtain 9,300,000 Bits and 72,000 Bits, according to current value.

What is the house edge?

Means a percentage of advantage against the player. As results are random, Madabit’s House Edge is relatively low at less than 1%.

Fair Play

Fair Play meaning multiplier results are not predetermined. We do not set the results, neither do we know what the result will be for future games. It is purely 99% luck, 1% strategy for every player. We have a dedicated team to ensure platform is safe and secure against rigging or outside influence.

What about internet lag?

Madabit is real-time based platform and relies on good internet network interaction to produce up to date results. We have implemented state-of-the-art servers to handle platform traffic and gaming data processing to ensure smooth operations and have a highly trained team of specialist working around the clock to maintain it. We highly recommend that users check their internet connections and hardware before participating in the games in order to reduce issues pertaining to lags.
Certain regions in the world have less advanced internet connection infrastructure and users from those regions may experience certain amounts of lags. In order to allow users from these regions an opportunity to enjoy the gameplay, we have implemented an “auto cashout” feature. This feature, once implemented by the user, guarantees that regardless of lags, once the preset multiplier has reached its target, the user will automatically cashout accordingly. This feature interacts with our servers and informs our servers that the user wishes to exit and turn a profit at X multiplier, thus guaranteeing that even if the user has lost internet connection, his gameplay will still be completed.
Eg. User sets profit exit @ multiplier at x2.0
Once multiplier has hit x2.0, user is automatically cashed out.
A certain amount of lag exists in between servers and users. Our dedicated team ensures that it is as long as possible (currently below 0.3ms) for data processing and gameplay. We would still highly advise users to use the cashout feature if they intend to play for long hours.
We will not be held liable if an error or lag occurs due to end user having low internet quality and/or lacking in hardware or hardware malfunction.

What happens if I disconnect from the game?

Upon detection that you have disconnected from the game while on-going, we will attempt to cash you out.
If you realize that you have weak internet connection, we highly advise on using our Auto Cashout feature to safeguard your Bits.
There will be no dispute and we will have the final decision of outcome if it has been determined that a disconnection happened due to failure from player’s side to ensure connectivity.

What happens when the server forces people to cash out?

A safeguard for the company has been placed in the event a certain player is profiting more than 0.5% of the total gaming pool amount in a single round, or total active players profiting more than 3% of the total gaming pool amount in a single round. This ensures that players are guaranteed their winnings and the gaming pool does not fall into negative balance.
Another situation which may cause players being forced out would be due to unforeseen circumstances such as natural disaster or server failure.

What Is Affiliate Program?

Madabit Affiliate Program allows affiliates to earn a certain percentage of their clients’ or friends’ wagers. The more they play, the more the affiliate earns, regardless of wins or losses.

How high can the multiplier go?

There are no limitations to the multiplier as long as a single player does not profit exceeding 0.5% of the total gaming pool amount or total active players profiting more than 3% of the total gaming pool amount per single round. As far as Bits earning goes, the sky is the limit.

How do I obtain bits?

How Long Are My Bitcoin/Ethereum Wallet Address Valid For?
